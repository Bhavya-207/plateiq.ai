Know Your Plate. Eat Smarter.

PlateIQ is an AI-powered web application that helps users understand the nutritional value of their meals by simply uploading a food image. The platform analyzes the meal, identifies food items, estimates nutritional information, and provides personalized health recommendations in a simple and user-friendly interface.

## рҹ“Ң Problem Statement

Many people struggle to estimate the nutritional value of their daily meals, especially traditional Indian dishes. Existing nutrition apps often require manual food logging, which is time-consuming and inconvenient.

PlateIQ aims to simplify this process by allowing users to upload a meal image and receive AI-powered nutritional insights within seconds.
